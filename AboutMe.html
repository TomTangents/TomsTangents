About me and The Purpose 

 

The purpose of my blog is to create a platform to bring together a community of individuals that possibly share a passion of a variety of interests of mine that includes cars, knife collecting, competitive gaming scene, and other niches of mine that I would like to discuss. As for my personal reasons, I want to make a personal blog because I want to use this platform to express myself as a person and communicate my personal thoughts in a unique way. In an emotional sense, this personal blog would also be a therapeutic outlet for me; sort of like a diary.
